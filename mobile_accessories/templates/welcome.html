About TeknixorDV

TeknixorDV was born out of a need for specialized inventory management solutions for mobile accessory businesses. We understand the unique challenges of managing cases, chargers, screen protectors, and other accessories across multiple brands and device models.

Our platform is designed by industry veterans who have worked in mobile retail for over a decade. We've built the tools we wish we had when running our own businesses.

Today, TeknixorDV powers hundreds of mobile accessory businesses worldwide, from small retailers to large wholesalers and distributors.
